Products & Quality

100% authentic guarantee! All our perfumes are:

  • Sourced directly from authorized distributors
  • Original packaging with authenticity codes
  • Fresh batch dates for optimal quality
  • Backed by our authenticity guarantee

We stand behind every product with our quality promise.

To maintain your perfume's quality:

  • Cool, dry place: Away from direct sunlight and heat
  • Original box: Keep in original packaging when not in use
  • Stable temperature: Avoid temperature fluctuations
  • Upright position: Store bottles upright to prevent leakage

Proper storage can extend your perfume's life by 2-3 years.

Eastern Perfumes:

  • Rich, intense, and long-lasting
  • Based on oud, amber, and exotic spices
  • Higher concentration of fragrance oils
  • Perfect for special occasions and evenings

Western Perfumes:

  • Fresh, light, and versatile
  • Floral, citrus, and woody notes
  • Ideal for daily wear and office
  • Balanced projection and longevity

Watch sizing guide:

  • Measure your wrist: Use a measuring tape around your wrist bone
  • Small wrists (14-16cm): 38-40mm case diameter
  • Medium wrists (16-18cm): 40-42mm case diameter
  • Large wrists (18cm+): 42-45mm case diameter

Need help? Send us a photo of your wrist via WhatsApp for personalized sizing advice.
