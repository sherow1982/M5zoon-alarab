Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Emirates Gifts Store is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with the best possible service:

Personal Information:

  • • Name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • • Delivery address and emirate
  • • Order history and preferences
  • • Communication preferences

Automatically Collected Information:

  • • Website usage data and analytics
  • • Device information and IP address
  • • Browser type and operating system
  • • Pages visited and time spent on site

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to enhance your shopping experience:

  • • Order Processing: To fulfill and deliver your orders efficiently
  • • Customer Service: To respond to inquiries and provide support
  • • Communication: To send order updates and delivery notifications
  • • Website Improvement: To enhance user experience and site functionality
  • • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • • Secure Servers: All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • • SSL Encryption: Secure data transmission during checkout
  • • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • • Regular Updates: Continuous security system updates and monitoring

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • • Delivery Partners: Essential details for successful delivery
  • • Payment Processors: Secure transaction processing
  • • Legal Requirements: When required by UAE law
  • • Business Protection: To protect our rights and prevent fraud

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience:

  • • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • • Analytics Cookies: To understand website usage patterns
  • • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences
  • • Marketing Cookies: To show relevant content (with your consent)

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send you promotional messages about new products, special offers, and updates. You can opt-out at any time by:

  • • Clicking the unsubscribe link in any email
  • • Contacting our customer service team
  • • Adjusting your preferences in your account settings

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or website notification.
