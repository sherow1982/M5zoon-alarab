Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority - Easy 14-day returns with return shipping fees covered

Our Return Guarantee

At Emirates Gifts, your satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a comprehensive 14-day return policy with return shipping fees covered to ensure you shop with complete confidence.

Return Timeline

You have 14 calendar days from the date you receive your order to initiate a return. This generous timeframe allows you to thoroughly evaluate your purchase and ensure it meets your expectations.

  • • Return Period: 14 days from delivery date
  • • Return Shipping: Fees covered by Emirates Gifts
  • • Processing Time: 3-5 business days after we receive your return
  • • Refund Method: Original payment method or store credit

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • • Item must be in its original, unused condition
  • • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • • Perfumes must be unopened and sealed
  • • Watches must be unworn with original documentation
  • • All accessories and certificates must be included

How to Return Your Items

Returning your purchase is simple and convenient:

1

Contact Us

Reach out via WhatsApp or phone within 14 days of receiving your order

2

Return Authorization

We'll provide you with return instructions and arrange pickup at no cost to you

3

Package & Ship

Pack items securely in original packaging and hand over to our courier

4

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • • Opened or used perfumes and cosmetic products
  • • Personalized or customized items
  • • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • • Items returned after the 14-day period

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification to confirm receipt of your returned item.

  • • Inspection Time: 1-2 business days upon receipt
  • • Refund Processing: 3-5 business days after approval
  • • Return Shipping: Fully covered by Emirates Gifts
  • • Refund Method: Same as original payment or store credit (your choice)

Important Return Information

Return Shipping Fees Covered: Unlike many retailers, Emirates Gifts covers all return shipping costs. This means you can return items at absolutely no cost to you, demonstrating our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Exchanges

We currently process returns for refund or store credit. If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or model, please return the original item and place a new order for your preferred choice.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will:

  • • Arrange immediate pickup of the defective item
  • • Send a replacement item at no charge
  • • Cover all shipping costs both ways
  • • Expedite the replacement process
