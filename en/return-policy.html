14-Day Return Guarantee + Shipping Fees Covered

We stand behind every product we sell with our comprehensive return policy

Last updated: October 31, 2024

At Emirates Gifts, your satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a comprehensive 14-day return policy with shipping fees covered to ensure you're completely happy with your purchase.

1. Return Period

  • Return Window: 14 calendar days from the date of delivery
  • Shipping Fees: We cover return shipping fees for your convenience
  • Refund Processing: Full refund processed within 3-5 business days
  • Return Authorization: Contact us via WhatsApp to initiate return

2. Easy Return Process

2

Receive return authorization number and free return shipping label

3

Pack & Ship

Pack item securely in original packaging and schedule pickup

4

Get Refund

Full refund processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the item

3. Return Conditions

Eligible for Return:

  • Items in original, unopened condition
  • Original packaging and accessories included
  • Return within 14 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation)

Not Eligible for Return:

  • Opened perfume bottles (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items damaged by customer misuse
  • Items without original packaging
  • Returns requested after 14-day window

4. Refund Information

  • Refund Method: Original payment method or bank transfer
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days after item receipt
  • Shipping Fees: Covered by Emirates Gifts
  • Refund Amount: Full purchase amount including taxes
  • Currency: Refunds processed in AED

5. Exchanges

  • Product exchanges available for size, color, or fragrance variations
  • Exchange shipping covered by Emirates Gifts
  • Same-value exchanges processed immediately
  • Price difference applies for higher-value items

6. Damaged or Defective Items

  • Report damaged items within 24 hours of delivery
  • Photo evidence required for damage assessment
  • Immediate replacement or full refund provided
  • No return shipping required for damaged items
  • Expedited replacement shipping at no cost

7. Return Shipping

  • Shipping Costs: Fully covered by Emirates Gifts
  • Pickup Service: Free pickup arranged at your location
  • Packaging: Use original packaging when possible
  • Insurance: All returns insured for full value

8. Special Circumstances

Gift Returns

  • Gift recipients can return items without original receipt
  • Store credit provided when original payment method unavailable
  • Gift return period extended to 30 days during special occasions

Bulk Orders

  • Special return terms apply to orders over 10 items
  • Contact customer service for bulk return authorization
  • Partial returns accepted for bulk orders

9. Quality Guarantee

We guarantee the quality and authenticity of all our products. If you receive an item that doesn't meet our quality standards:

  • Full refund plus compensation for inconvenience
  • Free expedited replacement if available
  • Personal apology from our customer service team
  • Quality investigation to prevent future issues

10. Contact for Returns

To initiate a return or for any return-related questions:

Email: returns@emirates-gifts.com

Support Hours: 24/7

Response Time: Return requests processed within 2 hours during business hours.
